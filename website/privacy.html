Privacy notice

Your Data Matters

We value your privacy. This page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What we collect

  • Personal information: Name and email when you contact us.
  • Basic request data: Standard browser and server metadata needed to load and serve the site.
  • Direct contact details: Information you choose to send when you email us.
  • No first-party analytics cookies in the current site code: The static pages in this repository do not currently ship a cookie banner or bundled analytics tracker.

How we use your data

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • Operate and secure the site.
  • Improve the project and documentation based on direct feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

Data protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-party services

This website links to third-party services such as GitHub and RubyGems. If you choose to visit those services, their privacy policies apply there. The static site code in this repository does not currently embed a dedicated analytics or advertising script.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Ask what contact information we retain if you contacted us directly.

Contact us

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at bniladridas@gmail.com.
